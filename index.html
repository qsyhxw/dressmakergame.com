About the Game

Dressmaker blends mindful crafting with playful problem-solving. Sketch ideas in your notebook, pick fabrics in the shop, arrange pattern pieces to minimize waste, then sew neat seams and fit garments on the mannequin before sending customers off in style. As a dressmaking sim and sewing game, it rewards creativity and clean stitches.
