About the Game

Dressmaker blends mindful crafting with playful problem-solving. Sketch ideas in your notebook, pick fabrics in the shop, arrange pattern pieces to minimize waste, then sew neat seams and fit garments on the mannequin before sending customers off in style. As a dressmaking sim and sewing game, it rewards creativity and clean stitches.

FAQ

Is Dressmaker free?

Yes. It's a name-your-price prototype on itch.io‚Äîsupport the devs if you enjoy it!

Which platforms are supported?

Windows, macOS, and Linux builds are available on the itch.io page.

Will there be a full release?

The team has shared plans to grow the prototype into a fuller game with ongoing updates. The full version will be released on Steam (no date announced yet).

How do I fix the Mac "malicious software" warning?

Go to System Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí scroll down ‚Üí click "Open Anyway". See our detailed Mac installation guide.

What's the difference between itch.io and Steam versions?

Currently only the itch.io prototype (0.5.5) is available for free. The Steam full version is in development with more content and features. See full comparison.

How do I get 100% quality score when sewing?

Follow the dotted line precisely, slow down at corners, and align pattern pieces to the grain line (arrows pointing left/right). Full tutorial: Sewing Mastery Guide.

Can I play offline?

Yes. Once downloaded, Dressmaker runs entirely offline with no internet connection required.

Is there a mobile version (Android/iOS)?

No. Dressmaker is currently only available for Windows, Mac, and Linux. There is no official mobile version or APK.

How long is the prototype?

The prototype offers approximately 2-3 hours of gameplay with limited customers and dress patterns. The full Steam version will have significantly more content.

Where can I get help or report bugs?

Join the official Discord community or check our troubleshooting guide.
