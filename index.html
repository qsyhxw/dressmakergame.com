A Labor of Love

Step into a relaxing and creative world where you become the designer. In Dressmaker, you'll be given a prompt and your task is to bring a unique dress to life. Choose from beautiful fabrics, carefully cut out your patterns, and sew the pieces together to create a fashion masterpiece.

This charming game was lovingly crafted in just 7 days for the Cozy Spring Jam 2024. It's a testament to the passion and collaboration of its talented creators: elyaradine, sarandipityx, and raithza.
