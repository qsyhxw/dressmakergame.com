Safe Download Checklist

  1. Check the address: the page should be on elyaradine.itch.io/dressmaker.
  2. Click Download Now: itch.io may ask whether you want to name a price before showing the files.
  3. Pick the matching zip: choose Windows, OSX/macOS, or Linux based on your device.
  4. Ignore APK pages: there is no official Android or iOS build listed for the prototype.
  5. Avoid reuploads: do not download executable files from mirrors, torrents, or file-hosting sites.

Mac Download Note

The itch.io page says the Mac build is not notarized, so macOS may show a security warning. If you choose to run it, use System Settings > Privacy & Security > Open Anyway after confirming you downloaded the file from the official itch.io page.
