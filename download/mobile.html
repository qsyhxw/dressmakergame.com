Dressmaker APK & Mobile Download Guide (2026)

Quick Answer — Is Dressmaker Available on Mobile?

Currently, the incredibly popular cozy sewing simulator Dressmaker is not available on mobile devices. The development team (elyaradine, sarandipityx, and raithza) has solely focused its efforts on the desktop ecosystem. There is no native dressmaker android release, nor is there an iOS port.

Official APK Availability Status

If you are searching for a Dressmaker elyaradine APK to install natively on your Android phone, you will not find one. Cozy Lives and the Free Lives developers have not compiled the game into an `.apk` package. The game's complex physics and sewing track mechanics are currently optimized heavily for a computer mouse, making a direct touchscreen port challenging without an interface overhaul.

⚠️

⚠️ Warning: Unofficial APKs & Safety

Because the game has gained a massive following, several spam websites and unauthorized app stores claim to offer a “Dressmaker game download apk” or a “Dressmaker Mod APK”. These files are fake. Downloading them places your Android device at a severe risk of malware, adware, or data theft. Only download Dressmaker from official verified channels like itch.io or Steam.

How to Play on Android (Workarounds)

Even though there is no Dressmaker APK to install, you can still play the game on your Android phone or tablet using a game streaming app like Steam Link. Here is the safest workaround:

  1. Install on PC: Download the game to your Windows or Mac computer from itch.io, or eventually purchase the full version on Steam.
  2. Add to Steam Library: If using the itch.io version, open your Steam desktop client, click "Games" > "Add a Non-Steam Game to My Library", and select the Dressmaker `.exe` file.
  3. Get the App: Download the official Steam Link app from the Google Play Store on your Android device.
  4. Connect & Play: Connect your phone to the same Wi-Fi network as your PC and launch the game.
  5. Configure Touch Controls: Since Dressmaker requires dragging fabric, tap the Steam Link icon in the corner of your screen, go to "Layout Controls", and change the input behavior to "Mouse Trackpad" or "Direct Mouse". This ensures your finger acts perfectly as the cutting scissors or sewing needle!

iOS / iPad Availability

Just like Android, there is no official Dressmaker iOS port on the Apple App Store. However, because the developers support macOS, iPad users actually have a distinct advantage. If you own an iPad and a Mac, you can use Apple's built-in Sidecar feature to mirror the game onto your iPad and use your Apple Pencil to guide the sewing machine and cut the fabric patterns with incredible precision.
