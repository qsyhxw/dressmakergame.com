How to Check Before Assuming a Console Port

  1. Check whether Switch, PlayStation, or Xbox appears in the official platform list.
  2. Check whether the Steam page has changed from desktop requirements to a broader platform announcement.
  3. Look for a clear post from the developers or publisher before trusting a reposted claim.
  4. Ignore pages that offer console keys, ROMs, cracked builds, or cloud-console downloads without an official link path.
  5. Use the desktop prototype if you want to play now and your computer matches the supported platforms.
