Quick Answer — When Does Dressmaker Release on Steam?

As of early 2026, there is no official Dressmaker game release date for the full version. The official Steam store page simply lists the game as "Coming soon". In late 2025, the development team announced they had "gone quiet" to focus entirely on building the full game after it was greenlit by Free Lives.

Current Game Status (Prototype v0.5.5)

What's Available Now (itch.io Free Prototype)

If you don't want to wait for the dressmaker full game release, you can play the free prototype right now. Dressmaker demo versions (up to version 0.5.5) are available for Windows, Mac, and Linux. This prototype features the core sewing loop, basic customers, and a sketchbook.

Confirmed Full Version Features

Town Customers, Shop Displays & Advanced Sewing

According to the official Dressmaker steam game page published by Cozy Lives, the full release will massively expand the scope of the original prototype. Players can expect:

  • A Living Town: Meet a whole town of people seeking custom-made dresses tailored to important events.
  • Economy & Reputation: Fulfill requests to earn coin and reputation, and display your own designs in your shop window to sell off the rack.
  • More Fabrics & Decorations: Complete creative freedom with cotton, linen, wool, silk, velvet, buttons, bows, appliqué, and lace trims.

Developer Updates & Roadmap

Timeline of Announcements

  • September 5, 2025: The project was greenlit internally for full game development by Free Lives.
  • September 18, 2025: The official Steam dressmaker store page (App ID 4019220) went live for wishlisting.
  • October 3, 2025: The final prototype patch (v0.5.5) was released.

FAQ

Is Dressmaker free?

The current itch.io prototype is free. However, the upcoming full version on Steam will be a premium paid game.

Will there be a Dressmaker Steam Early Access?

The developers have not finalized their launch strategy. It's unknown if the game will launch in 1.0 or use the Early Access program.

What platforms will the full version support?

The Steam page currently lists system requirements for Windows, macOS (including Apple M1), and Linux (Ubuntu/Mint), matching the prototype's platform support.

Can I transfer progress from the prototype?

Because the full game features a completely rewritten progression system, town economy, and new sewing mechanics, prototype saves will almost certainly not transfer.
