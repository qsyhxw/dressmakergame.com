Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-11

DressmakerGame.com (“we”, “our”, “us”) is a lightweight fan site for the game Dressmaker. We are not affiliated with itch.io or the game’s creators. This page explains what information we collect when you visit our site, how we use it, and the choices you have.

What we collect

  • Usage & device info (via Google Analytics 4): pages viewed, approximate location (derived from IP), device/browser type, referrers, session metrics and events.
  • Cookies & similar tech: first-party cookies for analytics; third-party cookies for ads and the embedded itch.io widget.
  • Ad data (via Google AdSense): non-identifying information to measure ad delivery and, where enabled, personalize ads.
  • Voluntary contact: if you email us, we’ll receive your email address and message content.

How we use information

  • Operate and improve the website (diagnostics, performance, content decisions).
  • Measure traffic and effectiveness (aggregated analytics).
  • Serve and measure advertising through Google AdSense.
  • Maintain site security and prevent abuse.

Cookies & third-party services

We rely on a few well-known providers. Their cookies/pixels may read or set identifiers in your browser:

  • Google Analytics 4 (measurement): Privacy • Opt-out add-on
  • Google AdSense (ads): Advertising & cookies • Ad personalization settings
  • itch.io embed (download widget): itch.io Privacy

Your choices

  • Browser controls: You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings.
  • Analytics opt-out: Install Google’s opt-out add-on or use browsers with built-in tracking protections.
  • Ads settings: Manage Google ad personalization at adssettings.google.com or industry choices at aboutads.info.
  • Regional rights: Depending on where you live (e.g., EEA/UK/California), you may have rights to access, delete, or restrict processing. To exercise them, email us: contact@dressmakergame.com.

Data retention

We keep analytics and log data only as long as necessary for the purposes above and as required by our providers’ default retention policies.

Children

This site is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date reflects the latest version.
