Dressmaker System Requirements

Dressmaker is planned for Steam in September 2026, and the Steam page already lists minimum requirements for Windows, macOS, and SteamOS/Linux. Here is the current requirement sheet, plus what it means if you want to play the itch.io prototype today.

Quick Answer

For the Steam version, Dressmaker lists modest minimum requirements: 8 GB RAM, 1 GB storage, and integrated graphics at least around Intel UHD 630 or better on Windows/Linux. macOS lists Apple M1, 8 GB RAM, and 1 GB storage. Steam Deck has no separate Verified status shown yet.

Can My PC Run Dressmaker?

  1. Windows players: check that you are on Windows 10 or later, have 8 GB RAM, DirectX 11 support, and graphics at least around Intel UHD 630 or better.
  2. Mac players: the Steam page lists Apple M1 as the minimum. The itch.io prototype has an OSX/macOS file, but the page notes the app is not notarized.
  3. Linux players: the Steam page lists Ubuntu, Mint, or similar with i5/Ryzen class CPU and Intel UHD 630 or better graphics.
  4. Storage planning: Steam lists 1 GB available space; the current itch.io prototype zip files are much smaller, but leave extra room for extracted files and saves.

Steam Deck Notes

The Steam page includes a SteamOS + Linux requirements tab, which is encouraging for Linux-based devices. However, the page does not show a separate Steam Deck Verified status yet. Treat Steam Deck support as something to re-check closer to the September 2026 launch.

  • Do not assume Verified status until Steam displays it.
  • If you are planning for Deck play, wishlist the Steam page and check the store again near release.
  • The current itch.io prototype is a desktop download; there is no official Android or iOS version listed.

FAQ

How much RAM does Dressmaker need?

The Steam page lists 8 GB RAM for Windows and macOS. The Linux table does not separately list RAM on the captured Steam page.

How much storage does Dressmaker need?

Steam lists 1 GB available space for Windows, macOS, and SteamOS/Linux. The current itch.io prototype zip files are 115-128 MB before extraction.

Can I play Dressmaker on an old Intel laptop?

For the Steam version, compare your laptop with Intel UHD 630 or better graphics, an i5/Ryzen class CPU, and 8 GB RAM. If your device is below those specs, wait for more launch feedback before buying.

Is the Mac version notarized?

The current itch.io page says the Mac prototype is not notarized, so macOS may show a security warning. Steam requirements for the full version list Apple M1, 8 GB RAM, and 1 GB storage.
